Our Vision
1.Prepare Youths

Acts chapter 1 verse 8 “But you will receive power when the Holy Spirit comes on you; and you will be my witnesses in Jerusalem, and in all Judea and Samaria, and to the ends of the earth.”

First of all, we wanna begin our journey from our society , our birth mark , to country to country 1st to prepare the energetic youths in order to send youths in different nooks and corners of the world forming great spritual leaders who can lead the world.

2.Church Planting

There are a lot of places in Nepal where people are forced to do services in house and other many inappropriate places as seen in the long run. So, in order to maintain stability and support them, we wanna plant at least minimum size church.

3.Social Work Sharing is Caring.

Yet Jesus also said, “I give you a new commandment: love one another. Just as I have loved you, you must also love one another.” (John 13:34)

Looking after the orpants, widow, poor and the people including victims, persecuted Christians , to make them able to Stand economically and spiritually too so that no one will have to face the feeling of being isolated or neglected .

4.Bible School Children are the pillars of future.

To open bible school in different parts of the country and sent different youths in the nooks and corner of the world feeding and growing children in the spirit.
